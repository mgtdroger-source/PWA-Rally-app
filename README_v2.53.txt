STC Recorder v2.53 — missing-control submission option

Based on proven v2.52 GPS engine.

Change in this bake only:
- When every control has a time, submission continues normally.
- When one or more control times are missing, the app warns that controls may have been missed or a wrong route taken.
- The user may choose Go Back or Submit Anyway.
- Blank control times are preserved in the submitted results.

GPS timing and sequencing logic are unchanged from v2.52.
GPS diagnostics remain enabled in this field-test package.
